All skills cap at **level 99**.

## Prestige

Once a skill reaches level 99 you can prestige it. Prestiging resets the skill back to level 1, but grants a permanent bonus that stays even after the reset. Each skill can be prestiged up to **3 times**.

Every skill gains **+10% XP per prestige level** (so +10% at prestige 1, +20% at prestige 2, +30% at prestige 3). Combat skills also receive a flat bonus to their effective stat in every session:
